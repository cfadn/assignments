Deer Survival 2020

These losers will eat your passport! Feed carefully and to your discretion! Follow the instructions exactly!

  1. Hide your passport
  2. Hide your train pass
  3. Hide your deer feed flavored crackers

Deer in Nara, Japan, are well known for their bowing. Be warned, this tendency is deceptive. It is amusing the first time a deer bows before your majesty, hoping that you, almighty human, bestow a grainy cracker upon its humble fly swatting tongue. But lo, for when you bow and feed the deer a second time, it will sneakily assault you, for the humility was but a ruse the whole time!
